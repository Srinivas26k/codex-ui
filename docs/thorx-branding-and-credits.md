# ThorX Branding and Credits

## Product naming
- Product name: ThorX
- Internal engine attribution: Built on top of OpenAI Codex

## Can we rename Codex to ThorX?
Yes, you can brand your product as ThorX while using the upstream Apache-2.0 codebase.

Practical guardrails:
- Do not present ThorX as an official OpenAI product.
- Keep explicit attribution in docs/about/license sections.
- Preserve upstream LICENSE and NOTICE obligations in distributed builds.

## Credit language (recommended)
Use this in About and installer notices:

"ThorX is built on top of OpenAI Codex open-source components, licensed under Apache-2.0."

## Design source of truth

Core tone to preserve in ThorX UI:
- Warm dark near-black canvas.
- Warm parchment text (#faf9f6), not pure white.
- Restrained monochrome accents, minimal color noise.
